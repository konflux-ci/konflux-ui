# 0. Use Architecture Decision Records

Date: 2025-11-22

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Architectural choices were discussed in PRs without a durable record.

## Decision

We will use [Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)](https://cognitect.com/blog/2011/11/15/documenting-architecture-decisions) as described by Michael Nygard.

## Alternatives considered

- **Wiki only** — drifts from code; not PR-reviewed.
- **Code comments only** — insufficient for context and rejected options.

## Consequences

- Decisions are documented with context.
- Future contributors understand rationale.
- ADRs are lightweight and version-controlled.
